About


Beales Shoe Store is a new and exiting shoe store that was set up in the heart of Dublin. It was set up by the owner Leona Beale to provide the latest designs of shoes at an affordable price.


Beales Shoe's currently has just one store, but will be looking to expand in the near future to other locations around Ireland, and eventually the UK.


Beale's currently has most of the major shoes on sale from various popular manufacturers so our customers can be guarenteed a wide variety of products.
